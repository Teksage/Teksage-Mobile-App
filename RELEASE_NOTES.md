### 💳 New Feature: Auto Payment (Recurring Subscription)

Teksage now supports **automatic recurring payments** for monthly subscription plans, powered by Razorpay!

**What's New:**
- Monthly plan subscribers can now opt into **auto-renewal** — no need to manually renew each month
- A new **Recurring** badge is displayed on the 1-month plan card to clearly distinguish it from one-time plans
- Users must explicitly **agree to recurring payments** via a checkbox before proceeding — ensuring full transparency and consent
- The payment summary screen now displays an **"Auto-renews every month"** label for monthly plans
- Coupon codes are intentionally **disabled for auto-recurring plans** 
- After a successful auto-payment, the subscription confirmation screen now shows the **next auto-renewal date** instead of a day countdown


**Multi-Language Support for Auto Payment Strings:**
Recurring payment UI strings are now localized in all 7 supported languages:
Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam, Hindi, Marathi, and English

### 💬 Chat Limit for Free Users

To ensure a fair and sustainable experience for all users, **free (non-premium) users** are now limited to **5 chat messages in 7 days**.

**What's New:**
- Free users can send up to **5 chat messages** before being prompted to upgrade
- **Premium subscribers** enjoy **unlimited chat access** with no restrictions
- A clear prompt is shown when the limit is reached, guiding users to upgrade for uninterrupted conversations
